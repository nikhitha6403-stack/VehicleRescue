## 🚨 The Problem
Every day thousands of people break down on highways and remote roads. They don't know what went wrong and have no idea where the nearest garage is — especially bike riders who have almost no digital support tools.

## ✅ What It Does
- 🔍 Detects vehicle fault and explains in **plain language**
- 🗺️ Uses **GPS** to find 3 nearest garages in real time
- 📤 Sends **one-tap SOS alert** with location to garage
- 🏍️ Works for **both cars AND bikes**
